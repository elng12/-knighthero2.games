How to Play Knight Hero 2: Revenge?

Start the Game

Click the "Play" button above. The game will load directly in the frame. Click inside the game to start your quest.

Control Your Hero

The game features idle mechanics. Your hero will fight automatically. Your main role is to manage upgrades, skills, and equipment to make your hero stronger.

Progress and Upgrade

Collect gold and resources from fallen enemies. Use them to upgrade your knight's armor, weapons, and special abilities. Unlock new areas and face tougher bosses as you advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the game?
It's simple! Just go to knighthero2.games and click the "Play" button to start the game right in your browser.

What are the controls?
The game is an idle RPG, so most combat is automatic. You'll use your mouse to navigate menus, apply upgrades, and choose skills.

How does my hero get stronger?
Your hero grows by defeating enemies and earning resources. Use these resources to purchase upgrades for gear and abilities, which permanently increases your power.

What happens if I close the browser?
Thanks to the idle mechanics, your hero continues to fight and earn rewards even when you're not playing. Come back later to collect your earnings!
